Sommerfest includes our basic typeset.

Sommerfest is a version of the well-known typeface »Mesquite«, adjusted for use during the HBK-Sommerfest 2008. Thus, it doesn't have too many glyphs, just capitals, each in two different versions. All in all, it includes 83 beautifully shaped letters, just enough to tackle most simple uses.

X close Black Frituur designed by Heiko
featured in Fraktur Mon Amour

Black Frituur includes our advanced typeset.

Black Frituur combines the broken and edgy shapes of a blackletter font with the round and soft shapes of written script. Excessive opentype features allow for a wide variety of formatting. Contextsensitive features connect single glyphs to give each line of text a handwritten appearance. Contains medieval numbers, an alternate set of capitals, opentype-fractions, lots of ligatures, and, of course, the infamous »long s«.



upper case (A - Z in two varieties), lower case (a - z, containing contextsensitive varieties)

numbers (1 - 0), punctuation, Euro-sign and @-sign, ligatures

depending on which features are activated, different looks can be achieved

X close Rex Mundi (regular) designed by Heiko

Rex Mundi contains our advanced typeset. Rex Mundi is available in regular and bold.

Rex Mundi unites the easy readability in small sizes of the serif with the clarity and lightness of the sans. The serifs in the lower half generate a clear line of text, while the serifless upper halves make for a recognizable and beautiful overall appearance. Rex Mundi is the perfect typeface for running text, tackling display purposes with equal ease. Included are small caps, medieval as well as table numbers and some obscure scientific signs no one ever needs.



upper case (A - Z), including Small Caps

lower case (a - z), additional glyphs (Euro-sign € ...)

numbers (1 - 0), medieval and table, punctuation, scientific stuff

X close Druckerei designed by Heiko

Druckerei contains our advanced typeset.

Druckerei sports up to five different variants per glyph. A specially developed contextsensitive opentype feature replaces typed letters in a quasi-random fashion with one of their variants to achieve an almost handprinted appearance. A special "circle" glyph can be combined with any other glyph to produce circled numbers and the like. The light variant to this typeface will perhaps be digitalized soonish.



almost every glyph is included in numerous variants

X close Steelcut designed by Heiko

Steelcut contains our basic typeset.

Steelcut is available in 7 different variations: Backfill, Solid, Plain, Singleline, Doubleline, Shoenail and Edelweiss. While each of those "Cuts" can be used on its own and is in itself a complete typeface, they also can be combined. Since they all share the same basic layout including spacing and kerning, they can be layered in every combintaion imaginable to create unusual new typefaces. Two of the cuts are available for free, every other cut can be bought seperately or you could get the whole set at once. Additional cuts are planned. The cut Singleline is losely based on the free font Woodcut.
